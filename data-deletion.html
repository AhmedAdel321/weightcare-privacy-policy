WeightCare - Data Deletion Instructions

WeightCare takes your privacy seriously. Below are the instructions to delete your personal data stored in the app.

Automatic Deletion on Uninstall

All data in WeightCare is stored locally on your device. This means that uninstalling the app will automatically delete all stored data, including:

  • Your weight entries
  • Your desired weight
  • Any local settings

Manual Deletion

To manually delete your data without uninstalling the app, follow these steps:

  1. Open the WeightCare app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap "Remove All Records".
  4. This will delete all stored weight entries and your desired weight locally.

Note: This action only deletes data stored locally on your device. Data collected by Google Ads (AdMob) is managed by Google and cannot be deleted through the app.
